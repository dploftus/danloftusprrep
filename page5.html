A PITCH FOR MY DREAM JOB:

DIRECTOR OF COMMUNICATIONS FOR THE DENVER BRONCOS

What I'm doing, who I'm following, and what plan I have to get there

One day, I hope and plan to hold the title of Director of Communcations for the Denver Broncer of the National Football League (NFL). The Broncos are a world-class sports organization with a pedigree of success and a track record of positive branding. To this point, I have actively sought out volunteer, internship, and networking opportunities to grow my knowledge of the industry, so I am ready when my time comes. I have already connected with communications professionals in the industry, and I have interned at the league office where I learned about the "back office" function and duty of that organization. These conversations with pros, experiences in the field and hours of study have all prepared me to soon enter the sports communication field in an entry-level position. With the foundation I have built and the plan I have set forth, I believe I will be on track and suited to one day be the Director of Communications for the Denver Broncos. 

In pursuit of this goal, I have laid out what I am doing now, who I have identified as mentors, and what my plan of action is going forward to meet this objective.
